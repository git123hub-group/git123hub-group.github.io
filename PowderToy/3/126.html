Element number 126 (ARAY) usage:
  .temp = emitted BRAY .temp
    * use HEAT/COOL/PROP to change its .temp
  PSCN input: making brown BRAY (destroying emitted BRAY)
  INST input: can pass through conductors (except INST)
  when pass through FILT: changing BRAY color.
  when pass through ARAY/INWR/WIFI: do nothing.
  and more functions at https://github.com/temp-TPT-mod-1/The-Powder-Toy-mod1/blob/mod-1/src/simulation/elements/ARAY.cpp
