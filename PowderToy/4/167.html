Element number 167 (CRAY) usage:
  .ctype = .type of particle that CRAY spawns in
  .temp = .temp of the particles that are spawned.
  .life = .life of the particles that are spawned.
  .tmp = the "range" of the beam.
  .tmp2 = how much space to leave before particles are spawned.
